What is an IP address?

  • Internet protocol - unique address which identifies the host/client/server on a network or on the internet.

  • In the past years ipv4 (000.000.000.000) has become increasingly ‘exhausted’, which is why there’s been a migration to Ipv6.

What is a domain name?

  • www.domain.com

  • Any name used to tie an ip-address to an IP-address, to be resolved by the issuer/company of the IP-address.

What is URL?

  • The characters in the address bar and associated symbols meant to direct the visitor to a specific location.

What is DNS?

  • Domain name system

  • Used to associate an internet address with a typed name, to be resolved by the issuer/company of the IP-address.

What is the difference between HTML and HTTP?

  • It’s entirely different.

  • Hyper text markup language → Hyper text transfer protocol

  • Type of data being sent → Technology by which said data is sent.

  • Http allows sending more than just Html data.

What is the difference between the Internet and the World Wide Web?

  • The internet is host to more than just the websites from the world wide web.
